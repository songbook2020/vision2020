너도 나도(Just one you) A

너도 나도 이 세상에 하나뿐인
소중한 주의 형상대로 지어진 걸작인거야
때론 남들의 눈에 비친 네 모습에
두려워 떨며 특별하게 보이려 애쓰려하지
때론 너의 평범한 모습에
남들의 모습을 비교하며 실망하지
이 세상에 한사람 완전하고 특별한
하나님의 사랑받는 오직 한사람
그 누구도 없다네 널 대신할 그 사람
하나님의 고귀한 걸작 그건 너

One you, one me. No one else could ever be just like you.
You'll see everyone is someone No one else can be.
If you could see what everybody else sees in you,
then you would be gappy you were made just the way you're meant to be.
And when you think "what difference do I make?",
Would anybody's heart break, I wonder? Do I matter?
Remember there's just one you. Completely, uniquely, you.
Original through and through. There's no one else like you.
Nobody could take your place. Nobody can fill your space.
Nobody else has your face. There's just, just one, you.
